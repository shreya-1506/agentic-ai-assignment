Skill Name

ReconBreakRootCauseSkill

⸻

Business Objective

Automate the investigation, analysis, and explanation of reconciliation breaks by tracing discrepancies across source systems, transactions, journal entries, and accounting records to identify the originating cause and provide audit-ready evidence.

The skill must:

	•	Analyze reconciliation breaks between General Ledger (GL), subledgers, bank statements, operational systems, and supporting records.
	•	Trace discrepancies to their originating transactions, journal entries, accounting events, or source system records.
	•	Correlate data across both sides of the reconciliation to determine why balances, transactions, or positions do not match.
	•	Identify and classify common root causes including:
	•	Timing differences
	•	Missing transactions
	•	Duplicate postings
	•	Incorrect account mappings
	•	Currency conversion differences
	•	Posting errors
	•	Data integration failures
	•	Manual journal adjustments
	•	Reference data mismatches
	•	System processing defects
	•	Perform multi-step lineage analysis from reconciliation break to source transaction.
	•	Quantify the financial impact of each identified break and root cause.
	•	Generate explainable root-cause narratives supported by transaction-level evidence.
	•	Prioritize breaks based on materiality, aging, financial impact, and operational risk.
	•	Recommend corrective actions and remediation paths for each break category.
	•	Maintain complete audit trails showing how root-cause conclusions were reached.
	•	Support configurable reconciliation rules, matching tolerances, and accounting policies without requiring code changes.
	•	Enable finance and accounting teams to significantly reduce manual investigation effort while improving reconciliation quality and close-cycle efficiency.

⸻

Acceptance Criteria Alignment

	•	Root cause identified and cited for each reconciliation break.
	•	Supporting evidence linked to originating transactions and accounting records.
	•	Reconciliation breaks accurately classified into standardized categories.
	•	Financial impact of each break quantified and documented.
	•	Complete transaction lineage preserved from break to source record.
	•	Reviewer-ready root-cause reports generated automatically.
	•	Audit-ready evidence package maintained for every investigation.
	•	Investigation cycle time reduced while maintaining accounting control standards.

⸻

Key Outputs

	•	Root Cause Investigation Report
	•	Break Classification Summary
	•	Transaction Lineage Report
	•	Financial Impact Analysis
	•	Supporting Evidence Package
	•	Remediation Recommendation Report
	•	Aging & Materiality Dashboard
	•	Audit & Compliance Package
