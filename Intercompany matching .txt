Skill Name

IntercompanyMatchingSkill

⸻

Business Objective

Automate the identification, matching, reconciliation, and analysis of intercompany transactions across entities to ensure balances align, exceptions are quantified, and unresolved differences are tracked through resolution.

The skill must:

	•	Match intercompany transactions, balances, invoices, accruals, journal entries, and settlements across legal entities.
	•	Reconcile intercompany receivables, payables, revenues, expenses, loans, and transfer pricing transactions.
	•	Identify unmatched, partially matched, duplicate, and conflicting transactions.
	•	Detect timing differences, currency conversion variances, posting discrepancies, and data quality issues that create intercompany imbalances.
	•	Automatically classify reconciliation breaks and determine likely root causes.
	•	Calculate and quantify intercompany differences at transaction, account, entity, and consolidated reporting levels.
	•	Track aging of unresolved intercompany balances and reconciliation exceptions.
	•	Generate explainable matching decisions with supporting transaction evidence.
	•	Support configurable matching rules including:
	•	Exact Match
	•	Fuzzy Match
	•	Amount Tolerance Matching
	•	Date Tolerance Matching
	•	Currency Conversion Matching
	•	Reference Number Matching
	•	Multi-Line Transaction Matching
	•	Prioritize material exceptions based on configurable thresholds.
	•	Produce reviewer-ready reconciliation reports, imbalance analyses, and exception summaries.
	•	Maintain complete audit trails and traceability from matched balances back to originating transactions and source systems.
	•	Enable finance and accounting teams to accelerate period-end close while improving intercompany reconciliation accuracy and governance.

⸻

Acceptance Criteria Alignment

	•	Intercompany imbalances accurately identified and quantified.
	•	Unmatched and partially matched transactions automatically classified.
	•	Aging of unresolved intercompany differences continuously tracked.
	•	Matching decisions are explainable and fully auditable.
	•	Reconciliation results traceable to source transactions.
	•	Material exceptions prioritized according to configurable thresholds.
	•	Reviewer-ready reconciliation and imbalance reports generated automatically.
	•	Complete audit trail maintained for all matching, exception, and resolution activities.

⸻

Key Outputs

	•	Intercompany Match Report
	•	Unmatched Transaction Report
	•	Imbalance Quantification Summary
	•	Break Classification Report
	•	Aging Analysis Report
	•	Entity-to-Entity Reconciliation Report
	•	Root Cause Analysis Summary
	•	Audit & Traceability Package
