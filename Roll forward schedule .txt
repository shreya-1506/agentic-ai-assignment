Skill Name

RollForwardScheduleBuilderSkill

⸻

Business Objective

Automate the preparation, validation, reconciliation, and explanation of financial roll-forward schedules by calculating beginning balances, period activity, adjustments, reversals, and ending balances while ensuring full traceability to the General Ledger (GL).

The skill must:

	•	Generate roll-forward schedules for balance sheet and income statement accounts using period-over-period financial activity.
	•	Calculate and validate:
	•	Beginning Balance
	•	Period Activity
	•	Additions
	•	Adjustments
	•	Reclassifications
	•	Accruals
	•	Reversals
	•	Ending Balance
	•	Ensure roll-forward calculations reconcile to the General Ledger and supporting subledgers.
	•	Automatically identify unexplained variances, missing activity, unsupported adjustments, and reconciliation breaks.
	•	Generate audit-ready explanations for all material movements and balance changes.
	•	Trace every balance movement to its originating transaction, journal entry, subledger record, or supporting documentation.
	•	Support configurable roll-forward templates for different account types, entities, business units, and reporting requirements.
	•	Apply materiality thresholds to prioritize significant movements requiring review.
	•	Produce reviewer-ready schedules with supporting evidence and reconciliation summaries.
	•	Maintain complete audit trails, calculation lineage, and financial data provenance.
	•	Enable finance and accounting teams to accelerate month-end, quarter-end, and year-end close processes while improving accuracy and compliance.

⸻

Acceptance Criteria Alignment

	•	Beginning Balance + Activity + Adjustments + Reversals = Ending Balance validated for all schedules.
	•	Roll-forward schedules fully reconcile to the General Ledger.
	•	Materiality thresholds consistently applied according to accounting policy.
	•	All schedule balances traceable to supporting transactions and source systems.
	•	Audit-ready schedules generated with complete supporting evidence.
	•	Reconciliation exceptions automatically identified and explained.
	•	Schedule preparation time reduced while maintaining accounting control standards.
	•	Complete calculation lineage and audit trail preserved for every roll-forward schedule.

⸻

Key Outputs

	•	Roll-Forward Schedule
	•	GL Reconciliation Report
	•	Variance Analysis Summary
	•	Material Movement Explanation
	•	Audit Support Package
	•	Exception & Break Report
	•	Source-to-GL Traceability Report
	•	Reviewer Approval Package
