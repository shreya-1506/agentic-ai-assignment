Skill Name

NDATriageSkill

⸻

Business Objective

Automatically review, classify, and triage Non-Disclosure Agreements (NDAs) against approved legal playbooks and organizational risk policies.

The skill must:

	•	Analyze NDA documents and identify key legal clauses.
	•	Compare contract language against predefined legal playbooks and fallback positions.
	•	Classify NDAs into risk categories:
	•	GREEN – Fully compliant with approved standards and can be fast-tracked.
	•	YELLOW – Contains deviations requiring legal review.
	•	RED – Contains high-risk or unacceptable clauses requiring escalation.
	•	Detect deviations from approved language related to confidentiality obligations, term duration, governing law, indemnification, liability limitations, intellectual property rights, data protection requirements, non-solicitation clauses, and dispute resolution provisions.
	•	Generate clause-level explanations for every risk determination.
	•	Propose redlines and alternative language based on approved legal playbooks.
	•	Produce a reviewer-ready summary highlighting key risks, deviations, and recommended actions.
	•	Support configurable playbooks, fallback positions, and risk thresholds without code changes.
	•	Maintain complete auditability and traceability of all classification and recommendation decisions.
	•	Enable legal teams to prioritize reviews and accelerate contract turnaround while ensuring policy compliance.

Acceptance Criteria Alignment:

	•	Playbook adherence > 95%
	•	NDA classification accuracy meets legal review standards
	•	Clause-level risk explanations generated for all flagged provisions
	•	Recommended redlines align with approved legal playbooks
	•	Review cycle time reduced while maintaining legal compliance and auditability
	•	Complete decision trail preserved for every triage outcome
