Skill Name

KYCRefreshWorkflowSkill

⸻

Business Objective

Automatically perform periodic Know Your Customer (KYC) refresh reviews based on customer risk ratings, regulatory requirements, and profile changes.

The skill must:

	•	Identify customers due for KYC refresh based on configurable review cycles.
	•	Detect changes in customer information since the last review.
	•	Compare current and historical customer profiles.
	•	Identify material changes in ownership, address, identity documents, business activities, risk indicators, and beneficial ownership structures.
	•	Determine whether a refresh review can be completed automatically or requires analyst intervention.
	•	Generate a comprehensive change summary and audit trail.
	•	Support risk-based refresh frequencies (e.g., High Risk, Medium Risk, Low Risk).
	•	Produce explainable refresh recommendations with complete compliance evidence.
	•	Preserve regulatory auditability and reviewer traceability.
	•	Integrate with customer records and document repositories through downstream systems.

The skill should focus on ensuring regulatory compliance, reducing manual review effort, and enabling timely identification of customer risk profile changes while maintaining a complete history of all refresh decisions and detected changes.

Acceptance Criteria Alignment:

	•	SLA adherence > 95%
	•	Change differences accurately detected and attached to refresh cases
	•	Complete audit trail maintained for every refresh decision
	•	Risk-based refresh scheduling consistently applied
	•	Reviewer-ready change summaries generated automatically
